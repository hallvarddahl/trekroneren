    • Our story

    For more than 70 years, the staff at Trekroneren has served its costumers with attentive service. Trekroneren has costumers from all over the world, who come to enjoy one of Europe's biggest assortments of hotdogs. The recipes are from Norway, Austria, Germany and Switzerland, but all sausages are produced locally.

    The Trekroneren Hotdog Stand has existed since 1946. In 1978 Tor Gadeholt took over the business and decided that the price of a hotdog should be three crowns. Since then the name of the hotdog stand has been Trekroneren. Kenneth Skaar and Dag Hjellestad took over the stand in 1992 and still run the business together.
